Introduction to the Blackjack Game

In this game you will get two buttons i.e. "Start" and "New Card"
Here are the steps how to play:

  1. Start the game by clicking on "Start".
  2. The two random numbers will be generated and those will get added.
  3. Then click on "New Card" untill you get 21.
  4. If the sum is 21 then, will win the game.
  5. If the sum is above 21 then you will lose the game.
